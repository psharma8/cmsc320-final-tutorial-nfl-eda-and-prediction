NFL data analysis and prediction of Ravens' chances for either Wild-card or Playoff

On 13th November 2018, I went to visit my family and friends in Baltimore. My friends and I were talking about NFL.

As most of us are Baltimore Ravens fans, we would love to see Ravens to win superbowl again. However, our team is not doing very this season. Some of the friends think that Ravens will be done by end of the regular season and will not play the any further for these season. In contrast to those friends, there are other friends including me who think that Ravens still can get to playoff even though they will not be group division champion, Ravens still have chances to make it through wild card entry. Two or three days later, I decided to predict whether Ravens will actually make it to the playoff or wild card entry by the end of the month so I can show the result of prediction to my friends and tell them what will more likely to happend by the end of the regular season. Addition to showing this result to my friends, I can use is as my project demo for one of my Data Science and Machine Learing class. I apply some data science and machine learning technique to do this stuff. In order to do it,first I needed to find the data that can be use for my purpose. I've found data from github where author has uploaded play by play details of each games from 2009 to presents. the best part of this data is they updated every nfl week and are in csv files which is easy to extract the information. All the procedures I have done for this are file go_ravens.ipynb, you can walk through the code, prose and comment to understand the more in detail.

# Get weighted score for each column using the ratio

df['total_pass'] = df['complete_pass']+df['incomplete_pass']
df['total_game'] = df['away_win']+df['home_win']+df['tie']+df['lose']
# denominator cannot be zero so first set total to 1 if it's 0 before getting ratio
df['total_fg'] = np.where((df['total_fg']==0),1,df.total_fg)
df['total_pass'] = np.where((df['total_pass']==0),1,df.total_pass)
df['total_game'] = np.where((df['total_game']==0),1,df.total_game)

Libraries from sklearn is used to get prediction
Inependent Variables are (X) :
home-team
away-team
home game win to total games played ratio (total home game wins / total games played) of home team
average total scores (total scores / total games played) of home game
away game win to total games played ratio (total away game wins / total games played) of away team
average total scores (total scores / total games played) of away team

Dependent Variable (Y) :
home team win (binary if home win then 1 else 0) here a chance of draw is ignored, and count it as 0. It's very
unlikely to have draw and therefore winning is more important to get to the playoff or wildcard round. Hence, we are
only interested to figure out whether team win or lose (draw is counted as lose)
